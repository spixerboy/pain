Pain History

"Pain" primarily speaks about the concept of suffering and how understanding true pain is necessary to achieve true peace, often stating the famous line: "Those who do not understand true pain can never understand true peace.". He believes that by inflicting widespread pain, he can force the world to learn from its mistakes and achieve a lasting peace, highlighting his tragic past and the deep pain he carries from his own experiences.
